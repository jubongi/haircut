WHAT IT'S ALL ABOUT

From now until April 6th 2013, join me on my quest to raise $10,000 for cancer research. I have been growing my hair out for two full years to shave it all off in the grand (and ridiculous) culminating event that is JulianGetsAHaircut.com.



THE WAY IT WORKS

Two weeks of doing to change lives. Through a series of four "two week challenges", both you and I will be able to donate not our money, but rather our time, our energy and our hearts to some very deserving people. For the final two weeks, I put my next haircut in your hands (ok, your wallets) where your donations are your votes. The haircut with the most votes at the end of the two weeks will be etched/carved/cut/shaved into my head for ONE WHOLE MONTH! Your options for my haircut run the right side of this page. Haircut voting opens March 24th, so mark your calendars.



KEEP IN TOUCH

I'll keep you updated with emails, so you never miss a two week challenge, video or story. I promise to keep them intersting and brief as long as you promise to forward them to everyone you know. Pay it forward to connect our networks and together we're bound to do something great.

SUPPORT A GREAT CAUSE

The Strategic Initiatives Fund provides annual funding for MSKCC's leadership to direct resources to the most promising initiatives and pressing needs of the institution. This fund speeds the most promising work, thereby shortening the interval between the time new discoveries made in the laboratory are delivered to patients. It also provides seed money for vital new research opportunities as they arise and ensures the necessary means to attract, recruit, and retain the best and brightest researchers and physicians. Lastly, this fund supports novel research endeavors that might otherwise go unfunded.
