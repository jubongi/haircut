The Strategic Initiatives Fund provides annual funding for Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center's (MSKCC) leadership to direct resources to the most promising initiatives and pressing needs of the institution. This fund speeds the most promising work, thereby shortening the interval between the time new discoveries made in the laboratory are delivered to patients. It also provides seed money for vital new research opportunities as they arise and ensures the necessary means to attract, recruit, and retain the best and brightest researchers and physicians. Lastly, this fund supports novel research endeavors that might otherwise go unfunded.

GET TO KNOW ME

Hey hey hey! I'm Julian!

I will be graduating from Columbia University in New York City this May with a BS in Mechanical Engineering and sights on entrepreneurship.

When people ask me why I am doing this, my answer is simple: I have felt the emotional and physical pain of sickness but also believe in the healing power of joy. In the memories of Dawn Garcia, my grandma Mary, my uncle Seymour and everyone else fighting, help me raise as much as we can. If you do not already know, I encourage you to read my story here.

As the undesputed Class Clown and Most Involved of high school it is natural that I gravitated to a grand act like this. My love of new experiences– especially those that challenge me– further lead to the creation and development of this site.
